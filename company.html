About Funngro

Funngro is India's youth earning platform where teenagers and students can earn money by completing brand tasks, freelance gigs, and internships. The platform connects young people with real companies so they can build skills, earn rewards and start their professional journey early.

More than 60 lakh young users have already joined Funngro and worked with 5000+ brands through tasks like social media marketing, content writing, app testing, research projects and many more opportunities.

Featured on Shark Tank India

Funngro gained national attention after being featured on Shark Tank India. The founders presented the idea of helping teenagers become financially independent and career ready by working with real brands using just their smartphones.
